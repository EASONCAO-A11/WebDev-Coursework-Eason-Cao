Unit 3 focuses on guiding me through the full process of creating a client web page, starting from understanding who the client is to actually building the first version of their site. 
In Lesson 1, I've learn the overall structure of the project: how to select a client, how to read sample client profiles, and how the project steps move from research to design to coding. 
Lesson 2 shifts to communication skills, teaching you how to prepare for and conduct a client interview, ask empathic questions, define the client’s core problem, and brainstorm possible solutions before beginning the coding challenge. 
The optional sample client profiles provide additional practice by showing real interview notes from different clients, helping me to analyze needs and translate them into design ideas. Altogether, Unit 3 trains you to empathize with a client, define their goals clearly, 
generate creative ideas, and begin building a web page that reflects their needs.
