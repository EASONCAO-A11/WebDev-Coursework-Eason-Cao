When I visited some of my favorite websites, I noticed that most of them have navigation bars at the top of the page. The navigation bars are usually horizontal and clearly display links to important sections such as Home, About, Services, Projects, and Contact. Some websites also include drop-down menus to show additional links or categories. The formatting is generally consistent, with contrasting colors or highlighted buttons to make it easy for users to see and click. The navigation bars help users move quickly between pages and find the information they are looking for without scrolling through the entire site.
