I chose to explore Goal 11, which focuses on making cities inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable. Visualizing and designing the architecture of future cityscapes is a highly engaging concept, and this goal grounds those ideas in real-world metrics.

The specific targets for Goal 11 address foundational urban issues: ensuring safe and affordable housing, developing accessible and sustainable transport systems, and providing universal access to green public spaces. The site provides a wealth of resources, including data on how urban sprawl, waste management, and air quality impact daily life. The calls to action range from macro-level advocacy—like demanding integrated policies for resource efficiency—to community-level involvement, such as volunteering to build safe housing, supporting local businesses, or renovating local green spaces.

Design and Structure Appreciation
The SDG website manages a massive, complex dataset but maintains absolute clarity through a rigorous and highly effective design system.
